About

1

NI-NA WEEK is a commitment to stay off Instagram and abstain from shopping on Amazon for one week every month starting on the 15th.

2

NI-NA WEEK is a tool to practice conscious consumption and remind denizens of their own economic agency and power.

3

NI-NA WEEK is anonymous. There are no sign-ups and no check-ins — though we do encourage doing NI-NA WEEK with lovers and friends.

The Why

Screen Time

There is no shortage of evidence that too much screen time is affecting our mental health. Use NI-NA WEEK to give attention to that to-do list instead of your hand robot.

Social Time

Quality social interactions are good for the mind and the body — that's just science. Your crush isn't getting the hint from all those likes and emojis. Use NI-NA WEEK to set up a date with your potential boo instead.

The Environment

Data centers and distribution centers are putting stress on the environment. Sustainable energy production and supply chains simply can't keep up with instant gratification culture. Use NI-NA WEEK to go outside without telling the whole internet about it.

Economic Empowerment

Monopolies exacerbate inequality, with detrimental impacts to economies and communities. Instagram’s owner, Meta, and Amazon have faced investigations for their (illegal) antitrust practices. Use NI-NA WEEK to spread attention and resources to other companies intentionally.

FAQ

Why Instagram and Amazon?

We chose Instagram and Amazon for a few reasons:

  • They don't provide anything essential. You can share media outside of Insta and buy whatever you need somewhere else on the internet.
  • They dodge paying federal corporate income tax.
  • They are intentionally designed to be addictive and have corporate histories of unethical, if not illegal, behavior in pursuit of profits.

What if I don't use Instagram or Amazon?

We also chose them because "NI-NA" has a nice ring to it. But NI-NA WEEK is just a tool — a reminder to consume more consciously for a week every month. Choose another platform or pair to skip. For brownie points, pick something under the Meta or Amazon umbrellas (Facebook, Threads, WhatsApp, Whole Foods, Prime Video).

What if there are other platforms I'm more concerned about overusing?

We encourage you to skip those instead — or in addition to — Insta and Amazon. NI-NA WEEK is meant to be a tool: a reminder to consume and give attention a bit more consciously for a week every month.

When you say "Amazon", does that include Whole Foods?

Ideally, yes, but it's your NI-NA WEEK. We won't be checking in. Maybe start with skipping amazon.com and after a few months, add in a break from Whole Foods. Small changes are the best way to start a new habit — start as small as you need to and build from there.

What if I want to start my NI-NA week early or late?

By all means!

Do you have any tips for a successful NI-NA WEEK?

Obvs, yes. So glad you asked.

  1. Tell your peeps in advance that you won't be on Insta.
  2. If you meet someone you like just before NI-NA WEEK, get their actual phone number, not their Insta handle.
  3. Buy what you normally would during NI-NA WEEK — just not on Amazon. Extra points if you support a store that doesn't dodge taxes and runs their business ethically.
  4. Recruit someone to do NI-NA WEEK with you and help keep you honest.
  5. Hit us up on BlueSky with any questions, comments, concerns.
